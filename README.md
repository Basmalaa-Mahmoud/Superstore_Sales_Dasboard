# Superstore_Sales_Dasboard

## Project Overview
This project analyzes sales and profitability performance for a retail business using the Superstore Sales Dataset.
The goal of the analysis is to evaluate overall business growth, identify top-performing categories and regions, and track year-over-year performance using key KPI

## Tools:
- Power BI
- Power Query (data cleaning and transformation)
- DAX (measures and KPIs)
- Interactive Dashboards & Visualizations

## Analysis Steps
1. Cleaned and transformed data using Power Query
2. Handled data type issues, especially date columns
3. Created calculated measures for Sales and Profit
4. Built Year-over-Year (YoY) performance KPIs
5. Designed interactive dashboards with filters and slicers
6. Analyzed sales performance by category, region, and state


## Key KPIs
- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Sales Year-over-Year Change
- Profit Year-over-Year Change
Both Sales YoY and Profit YoY KPIs showed a positive increase, indicating overall business growth


## Key Insights
- Overall sales and business performance are increasing year over year
- Technology is the most popular and best-performing category
- The West region generates the highest sales and profit
- California is the top state for sales and profits, particularly for Technology products
- Year-over-Year analysis shows positive growth in both Sales and Profit


## Gained a deeper understanding of:
  - When to use measures vs calculated columns
  - Building YoY KPIs without a full date table
  - Structuring dashboards from a business perspective
